Spaghetti and Meatball Recipe

Description

Meatballs made with ground beef and herbs mixed in tomato sauce and angel hair pasta

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Oregano
  • Tomato sauce cans
  • Tomato paste can
  • White bread
  • Angel hair pasta
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Garlic Cloves

Instructions

  1. Soak bread with water
  2. Mash ground beef with eggs and oregano
  3. Form meat into balls
  4. Heat diced onions and minced garlic cloves in pot
  5. Pour tomato sauce in pot
  6. Add tomato paste to sauce
  7. Add meatballs to sauce
  8. Heat pot for 45 minutes
  9. Heat pasta in boiling water for 12 minutes
